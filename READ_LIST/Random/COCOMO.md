### 🔢 In Basic COCOMO, the formula is:

**Effort (PM)** = **a × (KLOC)^b**

Where:

- **PM** = Person-Months (effort)
    
- **KLOC** = Thousands of Lines of Code
    
- **a and b** = constants that depend on the **project type**
    

---

### 🧱 Constants for Basic COCOMO:

|Project Type|a|b|
|---|---|---|
|Organic|2.4|1.05|
|Semi-Detached|3.0|1.12|
|Embedded|3.6|1.20|

---

### 📌 So:

- **2.4 and 1.05** → only for **Organic**
    
- **3.0 and 1.12** → for more complex/semi-detached systems
    
- **3.6 and 1.20** → for embedded, high-risk systems
    

You choose the values based on the nature of the project.

## 3. Key Findings

  

### Order Patterns

- **Peak Hours**: Ordering activity peaks in the late evening (18:00 - 21:00), aligning with dinner time.

- **Amount**: The dataset contains some high-value outliers up to ₹11,971, though the median is ₹833.

  

### Delivery Performance

- **Duration**: The typical delivery takes ~55 minutes. There is a long tail of deliveries taking over 100 minutes.

- **Efficiency**: Approximately 64% of deliveries are marked as "Late" based on the `is_late` feature logic from the preprocessing step.

  

### Customer Engagement

- **Churn Rate**: The observed churn rate is approximately 12%.

- **Premium Status**: ~25% of the customers in the dataset are premium members.

  

### Demand Consistency

- The daily demand is relatively stable, fluctuating between 60 and 80 orders per day for the overall system.
